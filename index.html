Short Bio

I'm currently a research assistant at the National University of Singapore (NUS), NExT++ Research Center. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with specializations in Artificial Intelligence and Multimedia Information Retrieval from NUS in June 2022, and I was advised by Prof. Chua Tat Seng and worked closly with Prof. Liao Lizi.

My research interests lie at the intersection of multimedia analysis, natural language processing, graph learning, and computational social science. I have worked on their applications such as conversational search systems and temporal knowledge graphs. Specifically, I aim at developing models that can reason over multimodal knowledge, such as textual, visual, audio and graph information, and generate appropriate interpretations, predictions, and responses.

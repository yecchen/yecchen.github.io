Short Bio

I am currently a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at UCLA, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Wei Wang. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore (NUS), where I joined the NExT Research Center advised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing and Data Mining, with the current focus on reasoning over large language models and knowledge graphs.
